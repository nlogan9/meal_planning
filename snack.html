Snack

Hummus, Veggie & Edamame Snack Boxes

5 servings ~230 cal ~14.5g protein ~11g fiber

Ingredients

  • 1.25 cups hummus
  • 2.5 cups baby carrots
  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 2.5 cups shelled edamame, cooked and cooled

Steps

  1. Divide hummus, carrots, cucumber, and edamame evenly into 5 snack containers with compartments.
  2. Refrigerate; grab one box per day for up to 5 days.
Swap in whatever crunchy veggies your 3-year-old likes best — bell pepper strips and snap peas work well too.
