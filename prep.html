Shared prep (do these once, then split across recipes)

Rice — combine into one batch

Cook 7/8 cup dry rice (about 140g) to yield roughly 2.5 cups cooked. Divide into: 1 cup for the dal and 1.5 cups for the fajita bowl.

Chickpeas — rinse and drain all at once

Rinse a total of 8 cups chickpeas (roughly 5 standard 15oz cans), then divide:

  • 2 cups — farro salad base
  • 4.5 cups — farro salad vegetarian topper (if using it that day)
  • 1.5 cups — fajita bowl

Black beans — rinse and drain all at once

Rinse a total of 4.75 cups black beans (roughly 2.5 standard 15oz cans), then divide:

  • 2 cups — breakfast muffins
  • 2.75 cups — sweet potato bowl

Garlic — mince all at once

Mince 8 cloves total, then split into 3 labeled containers:

  • 2 cloves — farro salad dressing
  • 3 cloves — dal
  • 3 cloves — pasta primavera
