Dinner — Option 3

Veggie & White Bean Pasta Primavera with Smoked Apple & Sage Sausage

3 servings ~490 cal ~36g protein ~10g fiber

Ingredients

  • 3 oz whole wheat pasta
  • 1 cup cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 0.25 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 0.25 cup fresh basil, torn
  • 3 Field Roast Smoked Apple & Sage plant-based sausage links, sliced

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat a skillet over medium heat and sear sliced sausage for 4-5 minutes until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, heat olive oil and sauté zucchini and garlic for 3 minutes, then add cherry tomatoes and cook 3-4 more minutes.
  4. Add drained pasta, cannellini beans, spinach, red pepper flakes, and seared sausage back to the skillet. Toss with a splash of reserved pasta water until spinach wilts.
  5. Stir in parmesan and basil.
Pasta is intentionally a smaller portion here than a typical pasta night — the sausage takes up a good chunk of the calorie budget.
