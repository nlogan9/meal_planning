Dinner — Option 1

Air Fryer Chickpea & Veggie Fajita Bowls

3 servings ~515 cal ~26g protein ~11g fiber

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups chickpeas, drained, rinsed, patted dry
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 0.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1.5 cups cooked rice
  • 0.5 avocado, sliced
  • 0.25 cup shredded cheese
  • 0.25 cup salsa
  • 5 oz Beyond Steak plant-based seared tips (half a 10 oz package)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Ninja Flip air fryer to 400°F. Toss chickpeas, peppers, and onion with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and salt.
  2. Split the mixture evenly between both baskets/levels so nothing is crowded. Air fry 12-15 minutes, shaking or tossing every 5 minutes, until veggies are tender with lightly charred edges and chickpeas are crisp.
  3. While the veggies air fry, heat a lightly oiled non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add frozen Beyond Steak and cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently. (Or air fry in a free basket slot at 400°F for 6-7 minutes.)
  4. Divide rice into bowls, top with air-fried chickpeas and veggies, seared Beyond Steak, avocado, cheese, and salsa.
Fully vegetarian — one dish for the whole family, no separate protein tracking needed.
