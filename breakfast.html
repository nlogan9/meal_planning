Make-Ahead Egg, Black Bean & Spinach Muffins

8 servings (2 muffins each) 350 cal 26g protein 3.5g fiber

Ingredients

  • 14 large eggs
  • 2 cups black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1.5 cups shredded reduced-fat cheddar
  • 0.5 cup diced bell pepper
  • 0.5 cup diced onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp black pepper
  • 11 oz Impossible ground sausage, cooked and crumbled

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin plus 4 cups of a second tin (16 total), or use silicone muffin cups.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté bell pepper and onion for 3-4 minutes until soft. Add Impossible sausage and cook, breaking it up, for another 4-5 minutes until browned.
  3. Whisk eggs in a large bowl. Stir in black beans, spinach, cheddar, the cooked veggie-sausage mixture, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Divide mixture evenly among 16 muffin cups.
  5. Bake until set and lightly golden, about 18-20 minutes.
  6. Cool completely, then refrigerate in an airtight container up to 5 days, or freeze up to 2 months.
Fully vegetarian, so the whole family eats the same muffin. Reheat 2 muffins in the microwave for 45-60 seconds.
