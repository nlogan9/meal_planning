Lunch — Option 2

Loaded Southwest Sweet Potato & Black Bean Bowls

8 servings Veg: ~482 cal / Chicken: ~477 cal Veg: ~15g / Chicken: ~44g protein Veg: ~14.7g / Chicken: ~8.8g fiber

Ingredients

  • 4 lb sweet potatoes, diced
  • 3 cups black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups corn kernels
  • 1 cup quinoa, uncooked
  • 1 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup pepitas (pumpkin seeds)
  • 2 avocados, diced (add day-of)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 8 oz precooked frozen chicken breast strips, warmed (for 2 of the 8 servings)

Steps

  1. Toss sweet potato with 2 tbsp olive oil, cumin, chili powder, and salt. Roast at 425°F for 25 minutes, stirring once (or air fry at 400°F for about 15 minutes, shaking every 5).
  2. While potatoes roast, cook quinoa according to package directions.
  3. Whisk remaining olive oil, lime juice, and garlic powder together.
  4. Combine roasted sweet potato, quinoa, black beans, corn, red bell pepper, and pepitas in a large bowl. Drizzle with dressing and toss.
  5. Portion into 8 containers: 6 as a full, even portion of the mix (no chicken), 2 at about 2/3 portion.
  6. Just before eating, top each bowl with fresh avocado. For the 2 smaller-base bowls, warm the chicken strips and add on top.
Scaled for having the in-laws over — 6 vegetarian bowls and 2 with chicken. You'll have a little extra base mix for Bodhi.
