Lunch — Option 1

Mediterranean Chickpea & Farro Salad (5-Day Fridge Hold)

6 servings Base: ~300 cal Base: ~10g protein Base: ~7.5g fiber

Ingredients — Base

  • 1 cup farro, uncooked
  • 2 cups chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 0.5 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1.5 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1.5 cups cucumber, diced
  • 0.25 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 0.5 cup red onion, diced
  • 0.25 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 0.5 tsp salt

Protein Toppers (add day-of — choose one)

  • Vegetarian: 4.5 cups extra chickpeas (3/4 cup per serving) — totals ~460 cal, ~19g protein, ~15g fiber
  • Chicken: 18 oz precooked frozen chicken breast strips (3 oz per serving) — totals ~443 cal, ~36g protein

Steps

  1. Cook farro according to package directions (about 20-25 minutes), then cool completely.
  2. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, toss cooled farro with chickpeas, feta, tomatoes, cucumber, olives, red onion, and parsley. Pour dressing over and toss well.
  4. Divide base into 6 airtight containers. Keeps well in the fridge up to 5 days.
  5. Just before eating, top each portion with either 3/4 cup extra chickpeas or 3 oz warmed chicken strips.
Keep toppers stored separately from the base and add fresh to each portion the day you eat it — that's what lets this hold up for a full 5 days.
